EV Charging Stations

Electric vehicles can be charged at various charging stations. These stations come in different levels based on speed and efficiency:

  • Level 1: Uses a standard 120V outlet, typically providing 2-5 miles of range per hour.
  • Level 2: Requires a 240V outlet, providing 10-60 miles of range per hour. Common for home charging and public stations.
  • DC Fast Charging: Provides 60-80 miles of range in just 20 minutes. Found at specialized fast-charging locations.

Note: You can find charging stations using apps such as PlugShare or ChargePoint, or by looking for chargers at popular locations like shopping malls and public parking lots.

Benefits of Driving Electric

  • Zero tailpipe emissions, reducing environmental pollution.
  • Lower cost per mile compared to gasoline vehicles.
  • Quieter driving experience with instant torque for acceleration.
  • Eligible for government rebates and tax incentives.
  • Lower maintenance costs due to fewer moving parts.
